Welcome

Care that feels like home

Belinda's Aftercare gives primary school children a safe, nurturing space between the end of the school day and home time. Run with patience and love by Belinda Rodgers, every child is supervised, supported and encouraged.

We help with homework, serve a healthy meal, and make plenty of time for free play and fun — so children arrive home happy, fed, and with their school work done.
