Look After Your Glow

Aftercare Guides

Follow these simple steps to protect your results and get the most from every treatment.

01

First 24–48 Hours

Keep the area dry. Avoid water, steam, sweat and makeup on treated areas while everything settles.

02

Be Gentle

No rubbing, picking or pulling. Let lashes, brows and skin heal naturally without interference.

03

Cleanse with Care

Use only recommended, oil-free products. Pat dry softly — never scrub the treated area.

04

Protect & Maintain

Avoid direct sun and tanning. Book your top-up or fill on time to keep results looking fresh.
